## Usage

    for dotty
    $ sbt launchIDE

    # run with arguments
    $ sbt "run -f hello"


    new for dotty
    $ sbt new lampepfl/dotty.g8
    $ sbt new lampepfl/dotty-cross.g8

    # launch vscode for dotty IDE
    $ sbt launchIDE
